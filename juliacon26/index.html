JuliaCon 2026 Minisymposium

Julia Global 2026 is taking place in Mainz, Germany. It will feature the JuliaMolSim Minisymposium on Thursday afternoon, August 13.

Here is a list of confirmed talks, you can click on the links to see more details about the speaker/talk on Pretalx. You can also view them on the JuliaCon 2026 full schedule by selecting the JuliaMolSim Minisymposium track.

See you in Mainz!

Schedule (Thursday, August 13, Room: Muschel — N2)

14:30–15:00 🎤 Charlotte Rickert, Daniel Kats — ElemCo.jl: A Julia Package for Electron Correlation in Molecules and Materials

15:00–15:30 🎤 Alexander Spears — ML-accelerated simulation of laser-driven hydrogen evolution with NQCDynamics.jl – Julia and Python in harmony?

15:30–16:00 🎤 Noé Blassel — Computing transport coefficients using Molly.jl

16:00–16:15 🎤 Hiroharu Sugawara — BoltzTraP.jl: Thermoelectric transport for the Julia DFT ecosystem

16:15–16:30 🎤 Bruno Ploumhans — Algorithmic differentiation and error control with DFTK

16:30–16:45 🎤 Henry Snowden — Simulation of light-driven hot carrier dynamics & transport

16:45–17:00 🎤 Matt Larkin — NQCDynamics.jl: A molecular dynamics platform for non-adiabatic systems

17:00–17:15 🎤 Ash Baldwin — QCEngine.jl: Electronic Structure for Nonadiabatic Dynamics

17:15–17:30 🎤 Michael F. Herbst — Extending DFTK.jl's features, but not its code complexity

The JuliaCon poster session is in the same evening, and several JuliaMolSIm speakers will present their package(s) there! Please see the list on JuliaCon 2026 website.

On Friday, there is a block of closely related talks in the General track, at room Muschel — N1:

15:00–15:15 🎤 Nathan Zimmerberg - What’s new with MEDYAN.jl: A Coarse-Grained Cytoskeleton Simulator

15:15–15:30 🎤 Benoît Richard - Building a Coulomb explosion simulation on top of DifferentialEquations.jl

[Coffee Break]

15:45–16:00 🎤 Fabian Pieck - RandomSequentialAdsorption.jl - Modeling Adsorbate Packing in Area-Selective Atomic Layer Deposition
